RC OFF-ROAD VEHICLE

THE CREATIVE ANGLE

Frugal Innovation & Material Science

TECHNICAL BRIEF

This project was a study in Frugal Engineering (Jugaad)—achieving high-performance results using non-conventional materials. The core challenge was maintaining structural rigidity while reducing cost by 80%.

ENGINEERING HIGHLIGHT

I transformed industrial PVC pipes into a high-tensile chassis through controlled thermal treatment. By calculating the friction coefficient of standard rubber vs. modified erasers, I engineered a high-grip wheel solution for loose-soil terrains. The integration of an ESP8266 allowed for a custom Wi-Fi control interface, bypassing expensive 2.4GHz radio hardware.

PROJECT RAKSHAK

THE CREATIVE ANGLE

Mechatronics for Human Safety

TECHNICAL BRIEF

RAKSHAK is a mechatronic response unit designed to solve the Critical Second delay in emergency scenarios. It serves as a bridge between sensor-based detection and automated authority notification.

ENGINEERING HIGHLIGHT

The system utilizes a distributed sensor network that monitors high-risk industrial parameters. When a threshold is breached, a logic-gate trigger executes a multi-channel wireless signal. Our focus was on System Reliability—ensuring the signal transmits even in high-interference environments, potentially saving lives through automated vigilance.
